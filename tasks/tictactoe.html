Problem Statement

Title: Print "Tic Tac Toe"

Description:
You are required to build a functional Tic Tac Toe game using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript where two players can alternately take turns to mark "X" and "O" on a 3x3 grid, and the game should detect a win, draw, or ongoing play in real-time, highlighting the winning combination when a player wins and preventing further moves after the game ends, all presented in a visually clear and responsive layout.

Constraints:

  • The grid must be 3x3 with 9 clickable cells.
  • Players should alternate between "X" and "O" starting with "X".
  • The game must detect and declare a winner or a draw immediately after each move.
